Rich Dad Poor Dad

Description of Book

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert T. Kiyosaki is a personal finance classic that compares two contrasting mindsets around money: one from his biological (poor) father and the other from his best friend’s (rich) father. The book offers practical advice and challenges traditional beliefs about wealth, education, and financial independence.
