My Favorite Hobby

Drawing and Sketching Cartoons

Drawing is something I’ve loved since childhood, especially sketching cartoon characters like Doraemon and Mickey Mouse. It helps me express creativity and relax after a long day. When I draw, I feel free to use my imagination and bring my favorite characters to life.

I often use pencil and paper for sketches, and sometimes add color to bring out the fun. I’ve even recreated characters like SpongeBob, Tom & Jerry, and Chhota Bheem. Drawing cartoons is both relaxing and rewarding, and I like to share my art with friends and online communities.

You can check cartoon drawing tutorials on YouTube.

Biography: M.S. Dhoni

Early Life

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as M.S. Dhoni, was born on 7th July 1981 in Ranchi, Jharkhand. Initially interested in football, he was a goalkeeper in school. But later, he shifted to cricket and quickly rose through the ranks with his incredible talent.

Achievements

MS Dhoni is considered one of the greatest captains in cricket history. Under his leadership, India won the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy. He is known for his calm demeanor, sharp decision-making, and finishing matches under pressure.

Dhoni has received numerous awards including the Padma Bhushan, Padma Shri, and Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna. He retired from international cricket in 2020 but continues to lead Chennai Super Kings in the IPL.

My Daily Routine

Morning

I usually wake up around 7:00 AM, freshen up, and get ready for college. I attend classes and take notes. I carry my sketchbook to draw during breaks or whenever I get a moment to relax.

Afternoon

Afternoons are spent attending practical labs or working on assignments. I grab lunch in the canteen with friends. After college, I take a short break, sometimes scroll through memes or sketch a cartoon.

Evening

Evenings are for self-study and project work. After dinner, I watch some shows, draw something new, or play a quick online chess game. I try to sleep by 11 PM to stay fresh for the next day.
